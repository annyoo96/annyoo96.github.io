Hey there! I'm Ann :) I am a senior at UC Berkeley studying Applied Mathematics with concentration in Computer Science. My Travel Diary is to share my travel experience and to reminisce on the good memories! Looking at the pictures I took during these trips make me want to travel back in time to enjoy fun experiences again. I'm originally from Seoul, Korea, but have lived in SoCal for about 10 years. Experiencing different cultures at such a young age, I was always looking for an adventure to another countries to find out how they contrast in culture. I have discovered totally different ways of doing things from travelling and also gained awareness of new cultures, customs, people and places! Meeting people from other places always make me realize that my world view isn't the same as everyone else's. Every time, I discover something new and consider fresh ideas I hadn't thought before, and that's what I like about travelling. My next stop is Paris in March, and I am super excited!
